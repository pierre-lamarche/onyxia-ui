Analysis

Analysis

source-map-explorer et webpack-bundle-analyzer plugins are provided by the project. They allow analysis of the bundle.

Dev

yarn
yarn analyze

A bundle-report folder, located at the root of the project, contains reports.

Deployment

Analysis reports are built continuously and published in summary menu.
